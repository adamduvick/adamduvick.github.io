# Kim's Coleslaw

## Ingredients

- 1 bag coleslaw
- 2/3 c. mayo
- 1/4 c. sugar[^1]
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1/2 tbsp. poppy seeds
- 2 tbsp. diced onions
- 2 tbsp. vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp. white vinegar

1. Mix!
